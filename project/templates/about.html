About this site

Wasmer Speed center is the main place where we track the speed improvements in the Wasmer runtime compared to other WebAssembly runtimes.

The runtimes that we compare are:

  • Wasmer (dynasm, cranelift and llvm)
  • Wasmi
  • Chrome's V8

This site runs on top of Django and Codespeed
